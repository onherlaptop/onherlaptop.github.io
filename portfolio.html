PAST WORK

Complete Website Content Creation for IZMO (as part of MezB Content Team):

I collaborated on creating complete website content for a website set-up software company.

I was responsible for carrying out in-depth research and crafting persuasive content for a wide range of industries using the service, from chartered accounting and legal services to health, wellness, and beauty businesses.

This experience served to sharpen my researching chops and taught me to craft targeted content that keeps the end user at its core.

Content Creation and Content Marketing for FreshlyGround:



I created blog content on digital marketing and CMS tips for FreshlyGround’s readership of business owners.



I also created and marketed social media lead magnets, including a workbook on creating high-converting post titles, which garnered over 13,000 social shares and thousands of inbound clicks and sign-ups to the agency’s content creation courses (post with total shares in slideshow photo).



Privacy Policy for FreshCo/FreshlyGround:



I re-wrote the company website’s privacy policy to reflect important changes, including GDPR regulations. The focus was on creating a clear, easy-to-understand policy that kept users informed while communicating the company’s dedication to protecting the privacy of its users.



Editing was also carried out for adherence to the Chicago Manual of Style.
